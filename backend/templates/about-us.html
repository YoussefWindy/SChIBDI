About Us

Learn more about our mission and vision at SChIBDI.

Who We Are

SChIBDI (Symptom Control Helper for Inflammatory Bowel Disease Insights) is a dedicated platform designed to help individuals manage and monitor their IBD symptoms effectively. Our mission is to empower patients with actionable insights and tools to improve their quality of life.

Our Mission

At SChIBDI, we aim to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers by offering intuitive solutions for symptom tracking, data insights, and personalized care recommendations. We believe that better data leads to better decisions and improved outcomes.

Our Team

Our team is composed of aspiring healthcare professionals, engineers, and data analysts who are passionate about creating impactful digital health solutions. Together, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of those affected by IBD.
